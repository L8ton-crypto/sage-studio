Common questions

The bits people always ask.

Anything not covered? Just ask on the call.

What's the difference between Starter and Studio? +
Starter (£300) gets you a polished landing page, logo and hosting advice. Everything you need to be online and taking enquiries. Studio (£500) adds extra revisions, image sourcing, Google Business Profile setup, basic SEO and analytics, and a faster three-day turnaround. Most clients pick Studio because the extras pay for themselves in the first month of leads.
How can it be that fast? +
AI tools handle the parts that used to eat days, so the build is hours, not weeks. The time I save goes into the parts that actually matter: brand direction, copy, layout, the details that make it look bespoke instead of templated.
Do I own the logo and the site? +
Completely. You get the source files, the deployment, the domain. Nothing's locked behind a subscription, no licensing tricks, no kill-switch if you stop talking to me.
What if I want more than one page later? +
Easy. Most clients start with Starter or Studio, see how it goes, then either add pages later or move up to Atelier, the custom tier for multi-page sites, full brand systems, print-ready logos, workwear and signage advice. Quoted to fit, you only pay for what you actually need.
Are you only working with Newport businesses? +
South Wales is the home base, but everything's done remotely so I work with businesses anywhere in the UK. Local just means I can also meet for a coffee if it helps.
What if I don't have copy or photos? +
Most small businesses don't. Copy is included in the price, written from a short brief. Stock photography is sourced for free where it fits. If you need original photography, I can recommend someone local.
